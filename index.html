The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

A timeline of major milestones in AI development

1950

Alan Turing publishes "Computing Machinery and Intelligence," introducing the Turing Test.

1956

The term "Artificial Intelligence" is coined at the Dartmouth Conference.

1980s

Rise of expert systems that mimic human decision-making.

2012

Deep learning revolution begins with AlexNet winning the ImageNet competition.

2020s

Generative AI and large language models like GPT-3 and ChatGPT transform industries.
